Trends Shaping People's Lives

Correlations Between Trends

Scatter plot above represents different trends shaping people's lives. Using data getting from US Census Bureau we answer on some popular questions:

1. Is there a correlation between smoke and income, age or poverty?
2. Is there a correlation between Lack of Healthcare and Income, Age or Poverty?
3. Is there a correlation between Obesity and Income, Age or Poverty?

According to our plot, states with lower income and high perscent of poverty have more smokers, more health and obesity issues. Low income explains lack of healthcare and obesity. If you can't afford a good quality food and insurance, you will defenetly get health problem.

Data set is based on 2014 ACS 1-year estimates from the US Census Bureau
